Welcome to NAD Business Solutions LTD

14 Years Experience

Founded in 2010, NAD Business Solutions Ltd is a locally based business entity that is committed to meeting quality real estate needs in Uganda. NAD Business Solutions core objective is to provide engineering solutions to the Real Estate Industry needs as well as boost quality and innovation.

We have a qualified dedicated construction team ready to deliver engineering services according to plan, on-time and on budget. Our professionalism, craftsmanship & service has brought repeated requests from our clients to consider providing continuous services in the real estate business in Uganda.
